## Course Overview

Served as Teaching Assistant for **Computer Networks** at The University of Queensland, offered by Prof. Dan Kim (February - May 2024).

The course covers fundamental networking concepts including network protocols, TCP/IP stack, routing, and network security.

## Responsibilities

- Conducting tutorial sessions
- Assisting students with assignments and lab work
