I am a PhD researcher jointly supervised by [Prof. Dan Kim](https://eecs.uq.edu.au/profile/1419/dan-kim) at [The University of Queensland](https://www.uq.edu.au/) and [Prof. Vireshwar Kumar](https://vireshwarkumar.github.io/) at [IIT Delhi](https://home.iitd.ac.in/). My research lies at the intersection of **adversarial machine learning** and **network security**, with a focus on developing and defending against adversarial attacks on network intrusion detection systems, IoT networks, and other security-critical applications.

Prior to my PhD, I worked as a **Product Engineer** at [Cognizant Technology Solutions](https://www.cognizant.com/), where I developed an AutoML-based ticket analysis solution. Before that, I was a **Machine Learning Engineer** at [TaiyoAI Inc.](https://taiyo.ai/) (San Francisco, Remote), where I built Bayesian hyperparameter optimization systems and managed deployment pipelines for large-scale time series forecasting models.

I completed my **Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science** from [VSS University of Technology](https://www.vssut.ac.in/), Odisha, with a CGPA of 9.21/10. I am a recipient of the **Prime Minister's Research Fellowship (PMRF)** and ranked **All India 800 (top 0.8%)** in GATE CS-IT.
